From the Volunteers menu, clicking on Volunteer Interest Form yields:

This form has two possible uses:

  1. A user that is already logged in to CiviCRM can update their own contact information if they have access to this form.
  2. Any web user (not logged in to CiviCRM) can create themselves as a Contact in CiviCRM, if the form is made publicly accessible.

First Name, Last Name, and Email are required fields. Providing a Phone Number is optional.
